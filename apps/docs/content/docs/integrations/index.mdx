Keep your existing website, brand, and landing page. Just rewrite the `.well-known/lnurlp/*` and `.well-known/nostr.json` paths from your domain to LaWallet NWC, and every user on your domain instantly gets a lightning address and Nostr identity — no migration, no redesign.

## Connectors

- [WordPress](./wordpress) — Drop-in plugin that proxies `.well-known` requests to LaWallet NWC
