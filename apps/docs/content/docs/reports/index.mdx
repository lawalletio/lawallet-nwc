This project is funded by an [OpenSats](https://opensats.org) grant, announced in the [Fifteenth Wave of Bitcoin Grants](https://opensats.org/blog/fifteenth-wave-of-bitcoin-grants) (December 2025).

We are grateful to OpenSats and its donors for supporting open-source Bitcoin infrastructure. This grant enables us to build a solid foundation for LaWallet NWC — from testing and security to the SDK, dashboards, and deployment tooling outlined in our [Roadmap](../roadmap).

## Reports

Monthly progress reports for the OpenSats grant (Fifteenth Wave of Bitcoin Grants).

- [Month 1](./month-1) — January 5 – February 5, 2026 — Backend infrastructure, testing, security
- [Months 2–3](./months-2-3) — February 6 – April 5, 2026 — CI/CD, JWT auth, Figma admin dashboard, landing split (v0.9.0)
- [Month 4](./month-4) — April 6 – May 7, 2026 — Monorepo, full Admin Dashboard E2E, user Wallet, Activity Log, OpenAPI 3.1 (v0.10.0)
- [Month 5](./month-5) — May 7 – June 29, 2026 — Remote Wallets, card system apps, plugin system, installer CLI, first stable (v1.0.0)
- [Month 6](./month-6) — June 29 – July 10, 2026 — NWC Payment Listener, card activation, deploy targets (Umbrel / Start9), Backup & Restore (v1.4.0)
